Proto-Germanic terms that indicate actions, occurrences or states.

For more information, see [Appendix:Proto-Germanic verbs](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Appendix:Proto-Germanic_verbs "Appendix:Proto-Germanic verbs").

- [Category:Proto-Germanic verb forms](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Category:Proto-Germanic_verb_forms "Category:Proto-Germanic verb forms"): Proto-Germanic verbs that are inflected to display grammatical relations other than the main form.
- [Category:Proto-Germanic auxiliary verbs](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Category:Proto-Germanic_auxiliary_verbs "Category:Proto-Germanic auxiliary verbs"): Proto-Germanic verbs that provide additional conjugations for other verbs.
- [Category:Proto-Germanic causative verbs](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Category:Proto-Germanic_causative_verbs "Category:Proto-Germanic causative verbs"): Proto-Germanic verbs that express causing actions or states rather than performing or being them directly.
- [Category:Proto-Germanic defective verbs](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Category:Proto-Germanic_defective_verbs "Category:Proto-Germanic defective verbs"): Proto-Germanic verbs that lack one or more forms in their inflections.
- [Category:Proto-Germanic impersonal verbs](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Category:Proto-Germanic_impersonal_verbs "Category:Proto-Germanic impersonal verbs"): Proto-Germanic verbs that do not indicate actions, occurrences or states of any specific grammatical subject.
- [Category:Proto-Germanic verbs by inflection type](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Category:Proto-Germanic_verbs_by_inflection_type "Category:Proto-Germanic verbs by inflection type"): Proto-Germanic verbs organized by the type of inflection they follow.
- [Category:Proto-Germanic intransitive verbs](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Category:Proto-Germanic_intransitive_verbs "Category:Proto-Germanic intransitive verbs"): Proto-Germanic verbs that don't require any grammatical objects.
- [Category:Proto-Germanic reflexive verbs](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Category:Proto-Germanic_reflexive_verbs "Category:Proto-Germanic reflexive verbs"): Proto-Germanic verbs that indicate actions, occurrences or states directed from the grammatical subjects to themselves.
- [Category:Proto-Germanic transitive verbs](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/Category:Proto-Germanic_transitive_verbs "Category:Proto-Germanic transitive verbs"): Proto-Germanic verbs that indicate actions, occurrences or states directed to one or more grammatical objects.
